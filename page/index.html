The word “Chasing” means “衣带渐宽终不悔，为伊消得人憔悴”. The suffix “1020” is used only to avoid duplication of names, and October 20th is my birthday. About me 🎓 I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Technology at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (since 2023), co-advised by Xiaohui Peng and Zhiwei Xu. Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Shanghai University (2019 — 2023). 🔭 My research focuses on distributed systems and machine learning systems.

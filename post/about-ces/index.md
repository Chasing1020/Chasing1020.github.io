# 1. Our Environment

> This is a time of prosperity and crisis: China's economy is developing steadily and positively, and the world is experiencing changes never seen in a century.

The annual spring recruitment and autumn recruitment are the barometer of the development of the computer industry, from last year's Tencent cabbage 40w high salary fried on the hot search, and then some time ago, the major Internet majors in the collective dive, due to the epidemic of the re-emergence of the side of layoffs while also locking HC, to today's President Liu Kiping issued an article: "internal manpower investment will be adjusted from the edge of the business to the core business, in the industry to occur fundamental changes in the industry when actively embracing change".

In the general environment, today's Internet is far less than 10 years ago, the so-called "wind and waves, pigs can fly", which Zhihu V Howard's comment is: "Now the Internet from the era of incremental into the era of stock. Everyone has no way to make the cake bigger, so they can only grab each other's cake".

The Mythical Man-Month, the bible of software engineering, points out that a large number of people and a short time can not shorten the software development progress, and it is dangerous to use "man-month" to measure the size of the work. Because the number of people ≠ efficiency, the way to adjust the organizational structure of the current major manufacturers is also very straightforward - leaving the most volume of people. In this environment, only a small pool of talent to dig, the Internet starting salary also seems very high.

If only to high salary / good employment and blindly choose computer, it can only be said to have entered the survivorship bias. In fact, an objective fact is that the average salary of students graduating from our school is far below the average level of Zhihu and Pulse. The best students are able to get offers from all the big companies, but the students who usually waste their time, even though they choose computer, often end up not as good as we think. The salary distribution of this industry is not normal but power-law distribution, only a small group of the best people can share the most cake, the variance of the future development of the same industry will be very large. If you will choose to enter the industry in the future, please try to become the front group of people.

In this regard, I think the reason for choosing this major should be "you love it enough", not "this major is hot". If you like the process of building a whole world of order through a process control (state machine) and have some interest in this major, you may want to read on.

# 2. About CES

The School of Computer Engineering and Science (CES) offers four majors: Computer Science and Technology, Intelligent Science and Technology, Cyberspace Security, and Artificial Intelligence, hereinafter referred to as Computer Science, Intelligent Science, Cyber Security, and AI, respectively.

In fact, the gap between these majors, as reflected in the professional courses taken at the undergraduate level, will not be that big, at least most of the basic courses such as data structures, principles of computer composition, operating systems, computer networks (i.e., the 408 subjects in the examination) are treated equally. Corresponding professional courses will focus on the study content, specific details refer to [2019 Shanghai University undergraduate professional training program](https://www.jwc.shu.edu.cn/content.jsp?urltype=news.NewsContentUrl&wbtreeid=1192& wbnewsid=31263) and [Class of 2020 undergraduate major training program of Shanghai University](https://www.jwc.shu.edu.cn/content.jsp?urltype=news.NewsContentUrl&wbtreeid=1192&wbnewsid= 32552).

In most cases, there will not be a significant gap between the daily academic communication of interdisciplinary students, because the skills you will use in your future work may not be as closely related to your current chosen major. For example, if you choose computer science and technology, you can still study artificial intelligence, and if you choose AI, you can study cybersecurity-related content. But look carefully.

- Computer science and technology: can be understood as the computer industry, the Polytechnic University class, belongs to the computer in the basic disciplines, both soft and hard, optional development direction range is very wide, the specific direction of research depends entirely on your interest.
- Intelligent science and technology: need to learn some signal processing, matrix algebra, brain cognition, operations research-related courses, across a wide range.
- Cyberspace security: a new major in my year, the scope of study is not limited to the so-called "cyber security", but actually also distinguishes penetration, reverse, cryptography and so on.
- Artificial intelligence: the research direction and professional course arrangement is also close to the wisdom of science, including mathematical logic, data mining, brain cognition, etc., it is recommended that students with good mathematics choose, need very good line generation and probability theory foundation, but also this is the Department of Mathematics and communications direction of students "to grab a job" one of the directions.

There is no absolute answer to the question of whether a specific major is better or worse. From a personal point of view, the teaching program of the new major still needs time to settle down, 10 weeks of short semester lumped together, even if you take 4.0 does not mean that you really learned the course. In the general environment, most of those engaged in AI-related need to improve themselves through graduate school, and the famous school + top will become the real offer harvesting machine.

Remember `what the school teaches ≠ what I want to learn`, you need to be clear about where your own future competitiveness in the industry really lies. Nowadays, more than half of the interview contents of large Internet companies are not what the school can teach you, but the fact that the teacher did not teach is not a reason why you will not.

# 3. The future
